DECISIONS:
- Chose llama-cpp-python over Ollama because it runs fully local without a daemon
- Went with GGUF Q4_K_M quantization as the best speed/quality tradeoff on CPU
- Typer chosen over argparse for cleaner --help output and type hints

NOTES:
- Model downloads from HuggingFace on first use (~350MB to ~2GB depending on model)
- Context window is capped at 2048 tokens to keep inference fast on CPU
- History pruning kicks in at 500 tokens to prevent prompt bloat
